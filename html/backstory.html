BACKSTORY

In the year 2224, the vast expanse of space, once a frontier of exploration and hope, has become an arena for survival and supremacy. The New Frontier, a sector of a solar system rich in resources and strategic positions, has become the focal point of a conflict that spans across the stars. Six formidable armies, representing the last of humanity's splintered factions, converge on this celestial battleground, each driven by desperation and the unyielding desire for a new beginning.

These factions, diverse in their backgrounds and motivations, find themselves in a deadlock over the control of the New Frontier. The sector, with its promise of a fresh start and untold riches, offers the only glimmer of hope in a galaxy torn apart by war and dwindling resources. Yet, the path to salvation is steeped in conflict; the New Frontier is not only a haven but a battleground where the future of human civilization will be decided.

With the universe watching, these six armies unleash their strategies, technologies, and courage in a relentless struggle for dominance. Alliances form and shatter like comet tails in the night sky, as each faction vies to outmaneuver the others. The clash is not just over territory, but for the very survival of their cultures, traditions, and future generations.

In the heart of this cosmic conflict lies the crux of their shared predicament: none can coexist peacefully in the New Frontier, yet none can afford to retreat. With every skirmish and tactical retreat, the fabric of their societies frays, leaving them with no choice but to fight. The stakes are monumental, for the victor secures a future for their people, while the vanquished face oblivion.

Welcome to Celestial Conquerors, where strategy, skill, and a bit of celestial fortune are your tools for survival. Here in the New Frontier, you will command one of these six armies in a desperate bid for supremacy. Will you navigate this treacherous cosmos to emerge victorious, or will the stars witness the demise of your ambitions? The fate of humanity rests in the balance.
